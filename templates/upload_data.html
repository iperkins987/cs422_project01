We're excited to help you analyze your time series data. To get started, make sure your files are compressed
in a zip file. ChronoWave accepts JSON, Exel, and CSV files.

To upload your data, simply click the "Choose File" button, and select your zip file. Once you've selected
your file, click the "Upload" button to begin the upload process.

We know your time is valuable, so we've made our upload process as quick and easy as possible.

Once your data is uploaded and prepared, you're ready to start analyzing and visualizing your results.
Simply navigate to the Performance Metrics tab to explore the power of ChronoWave for time series analysis
and if you wish to keep your results, simply download the data from the Download Data tab.
