Whether you are a data scientist, researcher, or simply interested in exploring patterns and trends in timeseries data,
ChronoWave offers a user-friendly interface that makes it easy to upload timeseries data, analyze forecasts, and generate insightful
metrics that let you know how your forecasting models compare to others in the community.

With ChronoWave, you can upload your data in a variety of formats like JSON, CSV, and Excel. Our advanced algorithms
can also analyze timeseries forecast data and compute a range of error metrics including Mean Absolute Error (MAE),
Mean Average Percentage Error (MAPE), Symmetric Mean Average Percentage Error (SMAPE), Mean Square Error (MSE),
Root Mean Square Error (RMSE), and Correlation Coefficient, allowing you to evaluate your forecasting models.

Start exploring the power of time series analysis with ChronoWave today!
